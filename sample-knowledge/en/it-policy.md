# IT Policy of the Heinz-Nixdorf-Berufskolleg

Valid for the 2026/27 school year. This policy governs the use of the school's IT
systems by students and teachers.

## Scope

The IT policy applies to all computer rooms, the school Wi-Fi, the loan devices,
and the school's local servers.

## Wi-Fi access

- The teaching Wi-Fi is called **HNBK-Lab**. The password is reissued at the start
  of each school year and is posted in the school office in **Room A101**.
- For personal devices there is a separate network, **HNBK-Guest**. The daily
  password is available at reception.
- Student data may only be transmitted over HNBK-Lab.

## Accounts and passwords

- Every password must be at least **12 characters** long and is changed **every
  180 days**.
- Passwords must not be shared.
- Accounts are automatically deactivated **30 days** after a student leaves.

## Printing

- Each student has a quota of **500 pages per term**. Anything beyond that is
  approved by the relevant teacher.
- **Colour printing** is only available in the library.

## Local servers and AI services

- The school runs two local servers, **Spark-North** and **Spark-South**, in
  server room **B114**.
- These servers host the school's AI and the internal knowledge base. Student data
  never leaves the school network.

## IT support

- IT support is located in **Room B112** and is available **Monday to Friday from
  7:45 AM to 3:30 PM**.
- Contact: **support@hnbk.example**, extension **230**.

## Data security

- USB sticks may only be used if they are **encrypted**.
- Personal devices may only connect to HNBK-Guest.

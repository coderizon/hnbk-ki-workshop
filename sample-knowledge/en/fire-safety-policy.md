# Fire Safety and Evacuation Policy

This policy describes how to behave in the event of a fire and how an evacuation
proceeds at the Heinz-Nixdorf-Berufskolleg.

## In the event of a fire

1. **Stay calm** and activate the nearest manual fire alarm.
2. **Close windows and doors**, but do not lock them.
3. **Do not use the lifts.**
4. Follow the **green-and-white escape-route signs** to the outside.
5. Go to the assembly point and wait for instructions.

## Emergency numbers

- Fire brigade and ambulance: **112**
- Internal security service: extension **555**

## Assembly point

The assembly point is the **North car park opposite Hall 3**. There, teachers carry
out a **roll call** of their classes. No one leaves the assembly point without
clearance.

## Fire extinguishers

- There is a fire extinguisher in **every corridor** next to the stairwells.
- IT rooms **B112** and **B114** have **CO2 extinguishers**, because electronic
  equipment is operated there.

## Responsible person

- The fire safety officer is **Ms. Keller**, who can be found in **Room A004**,
  extension **204**.

## Drills and obligations

- **Evacuation drills** take place **twice a year**, in autumn and in spring.
- **Fire doors** must never be wedged or held open.
